Goal

Making a blog application where users can register, login and logout. Every user can post an article on its own profile page, view a list of posts of everyone on the blog and view a specific post entirely including the comments. Every user can also comment on each post.

Website pages

Home page, choose from three menu options.

Login an existing user.

Register a new user.

When logged in, the page is redirected to your own profile page. Here you can add new posts and view all posts you made. This user didn't post anything yet.

If you checkout the blog, you'll see an overview of the posts of every user.

When you click on a post, you'll see this post including its comments, and you can leave a comment yourself.

When you're not logged in, you can only view the post and comments but not place a comment yourself.
